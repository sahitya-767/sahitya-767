On the path to customer success

4.0 6 months ago

Comments: Being provided with more knowledge about our customer's behaviour is a fundamental strategic tool to
understand our successes but also our failures. Gainsight PX helped us identify and map low engagement by customers, which translates into determining where customers needed assistance and what features must be improved.

Pros:

We love every in app interaction, particularly banners, sliders, and tours. We also really like the engagement and survey analytics available. Overall, this is a fantastic solution that will help you serve your client base more effectively.

Cons:

PX might be challenging to learn and master. It took us a while to master the product mapper and some of the other analytical tools. And we are still learning with the tutorials to gain insight on other/new ways the product can be used.

Why Capterra is free
